Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players each. The game is played on a field with a 22-yard pitch at the center, where one team bats while the other bowls and fields. The objective is for the batting team to score more runs than the bowling team

volleyball is a popular team sport played between two teams of six players each. objective is to score points by sending the ball over the net into the opponent's side of the court, while preventing the other team from doing the same. The game is played on a rectangular court divided by a net.

Kabaddi is a traditional contact sport originating in India, played between two teams of seven players each. The objective is for a raider from one team to enter the opponent’s half, touch or tag as many defenders as possible, and return to their side without being caught. it's called kabaddi
